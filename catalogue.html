Corporate Profile Sonoray Medical Systems
Company Profile

Established in 2004 in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, Sonoray Medical Systems has grown into a leading and trusted supplier, dealer, and service provider of diagnostic imaging systems across India.

We deliver an extensive portfolio of high-grade diagnostics, serving hospitals, diagnostics laboratories, and veterinary clinics. Our signature Sonoray DS Series provides state-of-the-art ultrasound machines that balance premium technology with accessibility.

Authorized SonoScape Partner

As authorized dealers for SonoScape, we bring globally acclaimed AI-assisted diagnostic ultrasound solutions and high-frequency systems directly to modern healthcare settings.

We are also renowned for our specialized technical service, offering component-level repairs for all major brands of ultrasound probes and transducers to maximize equipment life.
